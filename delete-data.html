Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like your data removed from our systems, please follow the steps below:

Step-by-Step

  1. Email us at support@yourdomain.com with the subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your WhatsApp number and user ID (if available)
  3. We will confirm and delete your data within 7 working days

If you uninstall the desktop app, your local data will be removed automatically. No cloud data is retained after deletion.
