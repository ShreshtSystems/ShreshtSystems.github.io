About Us

Powering a Sustainable Future

Since our inception, we've been committed to delivering innovative energy and security solutions that make a real difference in people's lives.

Shresht Systems, based in Udupi District, is your trusted partner in delivering cutting-edge solutions for energy efficiency, security, and smart living. Located at Harshitha Building, Ontibettu, we are committed to providing top-quality service and products for both residential and commercial clients.

With years of expertise, we specialize in a wide range of products and services designed to make your life more sustainable, secure, and comfortable.
