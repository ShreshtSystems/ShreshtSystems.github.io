Solar Energy Systems

Efficient & Sustainable Power for Your Home or Business

At Shresht Systems, we specialize in delivering advanced solar energy solutions tailored to your unique power requirements.

Whether you're looking to reduce electricity bills, minimize carbon footprint, or ensure uninterrupted power, our offerings — from on-grid and off-grid systems to hybrid setups — ensure reliable, eco-friendly performance.

Every installation is backed by expert design, certified components, and long-term support to ensure optimal energy output and system durability.

Our Solar Services

Solar & Electrical Installation

Our expert team offers end-to-end installation of solar panels and electrical systems, ensuring long-lasting performance and energy efficiency.

We focus on both residential and commercial installations with precision wiring, secure mounting, and full compliance with electrical standards.

Battery Energy Storage

Optimize your solar power usage with high-capacity lithium or lead-acid battery systems designed for seamless energy backup.

Our solutions help reduce grid dependency and improve energy security during outages or peak load hours.

Hybrid Solar Power Plant

A hybrid plant integrates solar with grid or diesel sources, offering uninterrupted power supply and enhanced efficiency.

These systems are ideal for industries and remote locations where reliability and fuel savings matter.

Off-Grid & On-Grid Solar Systems

We design both off-grid (independent) and on-grid (utility-connected) systems to meet your energy needs and budget.

These systems support net metering, battery backup, and power redundancy for ultimate energy independence.

Electric Vehicle Charger

We provide home and commercial EV charging stations integrated with solar energy to support green transportation.

Chargers are compatible with major EV brands and offer smart monitoring for charging status and energy use.

Solar Water Heater

Utilize the sun’s heat to provide hot water for homes and businesses, reducing electricity or gas bills significantly.

Our heaters are available in various capacities and work efficiently in both tropical and cold regions.
