Our Security Services

Dome Cameras

Ideal for indoor environments, dome cameras offer discreet surveillance and wide-angle views. Their compact design helps blend seamlessly with modern interiors.

Applications: Retail shops, offices, lobbies, and indoor corridors.

Bullet Cameras

These weatherproof cameras are perfect for long-range monitoring. Their visible shape can act as a deterrent to intruders.

Applications: Parking lots, gates, and long driveways.

HD Cameras

Capture sharp, clear footage even in low-light conditions. Great for evidence collection and detailed monitoring.

Resolution options: 1080p, 4MP, 4K

IP Cameras

Internet-enabled surveillance allows you to monitor your premises from any device, anywhere.

Features: Cloud storage, remote access, alerts

Solar Cameras

Designed for off-grid locations, solar-powered cameras are energy-efficient and eco-friendly.

No wiring or power source needed.

WiFi Cameras

Easily install these wireless cameras anywhere with a Wi-Fi signal. Access footage through your mobile app.

Ideal for homes and rental properties.

Biometric Security

Control access with fingerprint, face recognition, or retina scanning. Integrate with your main gate or office doors.

Used in: Offices, labs, secure zones.

Alarm Systems

Our alarm systems detect intrusions, smoke, and unusual activity—alerting you instantly.

Options: Sirens, mobile alerts, integration with police.
